I worked on this project in CS397: Rapid Software Prototyping. It is a web app called ClockIn made with React.

Never turn off your alarm and fall back asleep with ClockIn! ClockIn makes you solve a puzzle in order to turn your alarm off.

To you the web app, first you must sign in to see your list of alarms. You can set a new alarm or check/uncheck any of your current ones. At the top of the screen, there is a switch that determines the type of puzzle you will need to solve. The options are word puzzles where you need to unscramble a random word and math problems where you need to solve a simple random equation.

When the set time occurs, the alarm will start ringing and will not stop until you correctly solve the puzzle.

If you no longer want the alarm in your list, you can delete it.
